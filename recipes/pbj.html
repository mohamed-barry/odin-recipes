Peanut Butter and Jelly

Simple yet very effective food selection. When looking to gain some weight,eating a few of these a day will surely help with that,

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of break of choice
  • 2 table spoons of peanut butter
  • 2 teapsoons grape jelly or strawberry jam

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread
  2. Spead the jelly on the other side
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to make sandwich
  4. Cut off crusts if thats you style
