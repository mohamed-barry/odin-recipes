Chicken and Rice

Chicken and rice is a staple bodybuilding meal. It provides the protein and carbs needed to gain and refuel muscle.

Ingredients

    Marinade Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh
  • Lemon
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Garlic
  • Dried Oregano & Thyme
  • Olive oil

    • Rice Ingredients

  • Long Grain White Rice
  • Chicken Stock
  • Baby Spinach
  • Onion
  • Dried Oregano

Steps

  1. Whisk the marinade ingredients.
  2. Pour marinade over chicken and marinate the fridge
  3. Cook both sides of chicken in large skillet until golden. Remove from pan and set aside
  4. Saute the onions, spinach, garlic w/ oregano, salt, pepper and reserved marinade. Stir until spinach is slightly wilted
  5. Add rice and broth to skillet and bring to a summer. Place chicken thighs on top and cover the skiller. bake for 35 minutes
  6. Remove lid and bake for additional 10 minutes
  7. Let the chicken rest and garnish with parsley and lemon zest.
